WELCOME TO PUTT

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide underprivileged children in India with the opportunity to learn and play the game of golf by donating unused golf equipment and clothing. We believe that every child should have the chance to participate in sports and learn valuable life-skills, regardless of their background. By repurposing unused golf items and giving them to children in need, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of these children and promote the growth of golf in India.

WELCOME TO PUTT

What We Do

Our organization works to bring joy and the vast benefits of golf to more children in India. We collect used golf equipment and clothing through donations and ship it directly to underprivileged children in rural parts of the country. By repurposing these items for the better and giving them to children in need, we hope to make a positive impact and introduce more kids to the great game of golf.
